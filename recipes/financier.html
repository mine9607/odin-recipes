Fantastic French Financiers

Description

French financiers are a a classic bite-sized browned butter almond tea cake that you often find in a French patisserie.
The exterior is crispy with a delicate, buttery and nutty interior from browning the butter!

Ingredients

  • Unsalted Butter (115g)
  • Powdered Sugar (150g)
  • Almond Flour (125g)
  • All-Purpose Flour (45 g)
  • Egg-Whites (from 4 large eggs)
  • Salt (2g)
  • Vanilla Extract (2 tsp)

Steps

  1. Brown the Butter
    2. Place the butter in a small saucepan. Melt over medium heat. Continue cooking until butter is boiling
    and turns golden brown and smells nutty, about 8 minutes. Pour into a bowl and cool for 30 minutes.

  2. Preheat the Oven
    3. Preheat the oven to 350F. Grease the wells of a mini muffin pan by brushing them lightly with softened butter.

  3. Mix Ingredients
    4. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, almond flour, all-purpose flour, and salt. Add the egg whites,
    vanilla, and cooled browned butter. Whisk together until just combined. (The batter will be thick.)

  4. Divide the Batter
    5. Divide the batter among the greased muffin pan cups. Fill about three-quarters to the top.

  5. Bake
    6. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes or until the edges are golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
    Let cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Remove and cool completely on a wire rack.

  6. Devour Faithfully your Financiers!!!
